How to Download

Before Downloading? You might find it useful to read Frequently Asked Questions.

On Windows Click on the above link to the font and choose to save the font to the disk in some temporary area. Next, right click on the font and select "Install".

On the Mac OSX Click on the above link. If your computer asks if you want to save the file, answer Yes. This will open a Downloads window with the font in it. Next, double click on each font in the Downloads window. Another window will appear with an "Install" button in it. Press this button. In FontBook you will see the two font families.
